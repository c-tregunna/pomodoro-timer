What is this all about?

Think of this timer as your productivity buddy to help you get through the 80s. It chops your work into bite-sized, 25-minute focus sessions with little breaks in between to keep your brain fresh. After a few rounds, you get a longer break—kind of like leveling up in an arcade game. It’s simple, effective, and totally retro cool.
